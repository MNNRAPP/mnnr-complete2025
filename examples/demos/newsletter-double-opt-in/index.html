MNNR — Newsletter Double Opt-In Demo

Drives POST https://mnnr-app.netlify.app/api/newsletter. Submission is double opt-in — you'll get a confirmation email; clicking its link hits GET /newsletter/confirm?token=… and flips status to confirmed. 


  


  
After clicking the email link

  

    The confirmation URL hits GET /newsletter/confirm?token=…
    and returns HTML — flips the subscriber to confirmed and
    fires the welcome email. You don't need this demo to test it; just click
    the link in your inbox.
