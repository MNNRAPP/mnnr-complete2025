## ðŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

1. **Personalize the opening** - Mention their company name and industry
2. **Use their language** - If they say "kiosks" use "kiosks" not "vending machines"
3. **Quantify everything** - "40% reduction" not "significant savings"
4. **Create urgency** - "Pilot program for first 10 companies" (scarcity)
5. **Always give two options** - "Demo or info?" (not "yes or no")
6. **End with confirmation** - "I'll send that to [email] - expect it in 5 minutes"
