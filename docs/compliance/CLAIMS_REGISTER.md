## Purpose

This register catalogues every factual, compliance, capability, and positioning claim that MNNR LLC intends to make publicly (on mnnr.app, in pitch decks, marketing, or regulatory filings). No claim may be published until it has an APPROVED status in this register, confirmed by the designated Reviewer, with a valid Evidence Source and Evidence Location on file. This register is foundational for Ticket 0.5 (/trust page) and Ticket 0.6 (A50 Wedge Page rewrite).

## Approval Process

To move a claim from PENDING to APPROVED, the designated Reviewer (TOHID, LEGAL, or TECH) must: (a) verify the Evidence Source is a real, current artifact (URL, contract, certificate, audit report); (b) confirm the Evidence Location points to an accessible file path or repo path containing the artifact; (c) set an Expires date appropriate to the evidence type (certificates expire on issuance cycle, regulatory alignments on legislation change, positioning claims on annual review); (d) sign off with their name and date. Claims with expired evidence automatically revert to EXPIRED and Public Use OK becomes NO until re-verified.
